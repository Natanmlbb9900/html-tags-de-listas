Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetagle, or meats.

Preparation time

  • total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 larges eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter of oil
  • Optional filling: cheese diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
